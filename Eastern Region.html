Project

The “Qamar” project in Al-Baha is part of Saudi Arabia’s Vision 2030. It aims to develop the Al-Baha region into a comprehensive tourist destination. The project includes the construction of high-quality resorts and hotels, along with improvements to infrastructure, roads, and public facilities. “Qamar” seeks to boost tourism in the area, attracting visitors from within the Kingdom and internationally, with a focus on eco-tourism and heritage tourism.
